# About LUCA
L.U.C.A. Coaching by tj is a groundbreaking, innovative, nurse coaching service that specializes in providing comprehensive solutions for critical care skills enhancement and staff retention in the healthcare organization.

Our mission is to significantly improve patient care and staff satisfaction in critical care settings. We are committed to equipping novice healthcare professionals with the necessary skills, knowledge and support to deliver exceptional care to patients while ensuring their continued growth and success within their organizations.
